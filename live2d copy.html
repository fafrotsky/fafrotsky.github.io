Live2D Rigging

Basic Information

  • Live2D Rigging for VTubers intending to stream or record video content.
  • Client must provide rig-ready PSD or must acknowledge that further preparation/cutting may be required.
  • Listed price only considers the base model, additional items and complexity will increase the pricing.
  • The final product will delivered as a zip file containing the model file (.moc3) along with relevant files (VTS settings, emotes, animations, etc.).

Base Pricing

  • 500 USD - Simple Rigging (Chibi, Mascot, Bust, Scuffed, etc.)
  • 1250 USD - Half Body Rigging
  • 1500 USD - Full Body Rigging
