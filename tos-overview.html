Commissions

Terms of Service

Credit

  • Do NOT claim Faf's work as your own or leave the commissioned work uncredited.
  • Provide credit to Fafrotsky for commissioned works on Twitch / YouTube and Social Media.
  • Clickable links are preferred as opposed to just listing names. Refer below for proper crediting conventions:
    • For commissions used on Twitch, refer to Twitch's Artist Attribution page to assign the artist badge or emote credit.
    • In Twitch panels, use Markdown and link to either this website or Faf's Twitter or Bluesky profile.
    • In YouTube descriptions, link to either this website or Faf's Twitter or Bluesky profile.
    • On Twitter, the client can credit the creator as @Fafrotsky.
    • On Bluesky, the client can credit the creator as @faf.dog.

Reproduction / Redistribution

  • Do not reproduce or redistribute Faf's work.
  • For clients who are looking to resell their model, they must contact me before transfer of ownership.
    • In the case of a transfer of ownership, the new model owner must adhere to my terms of service.
    • Following this rule will allow for official recognition of the model's transfer and new ownership.
    • Officially recognized owner of models are allowed to commission Faf for model updates.

Intent / Purpose

  • The client must clearly state their intent for any commissioned works.
  • The work must not be used for any other purpose unless discussed and agreed upon beforehand.
  • Clients are prohibited from using Faf's works in NFT / blockchain related technologies, AI learning / sampling / training, and / or hateful / harmful / discriminatory conduct or content.

Personal / Commercial Use

  • Do not use personal use work for commercial activities.
  • Personal / Commercial use for artwork will be determined and negotiated upon by a case-by-case basis.

Deadlines / Slots

  • The client must understand that the estimated commission deadline / slot is not agreed upon until payment & confirmation.
  • The estimated deadline is as described, an estimate, please set aside additional time in case of delays and for model reviewing.
  • The finished commission will be delivered at or near the estimated deadline and will not go two weeks past the deadline unless the client is notified of any delays.

Showcasing / Privacy

  • Faf reserves the right to showcase commissioned work on social media & portfolios and to use the work as samples.
  • Clients may request for their commissioned work to be temporarily private for a fee in which such work will not be posted on social media or anywhere public until the model is revealed.
  • This privacy period will expire after 6 months of the agreed upon debut / reveal date.

Blacklisting / Service Refusal

  • Severe cases of misconduct from a client or potential client will result in a blacklist.
  • Blacklisted users will be denied service and the blacklist will be shared amongst Faf's creative peers.
  • Faf reserves the right to decline or drop a commission at any time if:
    • The request contains questionable elements (see: Intent / Purpose section).
    • The provided materials are found to be illegitimate (AI usage, tracing, stolen design, etc.).
    • Involved parties' (client and / or Live2D Artist) behavior during the commission is unacceptable.
    • Involved parties have been found to be problematic.

Refund Policy

  • Full refund allowed within 2 weeks of payment.
  • 90% refund allowed if client withdraws past the 2 week window as slot changes negatively disrupt operations.
  • Refunds will not be available for completed works under any circumstance.
  • For in-progress works, partial refund are available with pricing based on how much work has already been completed.
  • Rush order fee will be refunded in the case Faf is unable to meet the negotiated deadline on a scaling based on days past deadline.
  • Any refund or cancellation must be discussed before any drastic action is taken.
  • Unfair chargebacks (no communication or poor communication) will result in the client being blacklisted.

Commissions Overview

Payment

  • Commission prices are based on USD.
  • Commission payment is only accepted through Paypal invoice or VGen, payment through other means will not be accepted.
  • Payment is typically prompted 2-3 months ahead of when the commission will be worked on (earlier or later depending on queue).
  • Payments may be fully upfront or in parts: minimum of 50% down payment upon agreement, remainder on completed delivery.
  • Final product will only be delivered upon receipt of final payment.
  • Once the payment is confirmed, the client's slot will be locked in along with an estimated deadline.

General Process

  • Accepted clients will be set up in a queue. Queue position depends on requested deadlines, available slots, and model art readiness.
  • Rush orders may be negotiated upon will come at a fee (price & deadline depends on negotiation).
  • Clients will be notified when their commission has begun.
  • Upon start, commissions take from 3 weeks to 2 months to complete depending on complexity.
  • Some commissions may take longer to complete depending on Faf's personal obligations / complexity / necessary adjustments on Faf's end or the model artist's end.
  • Commissions are likely to be finished within the provided deadlines, however estimated deadlines are not guaranteed due to the above factors.
  • Clients will be notified if there will be expected delays.
  • Clients are welcome to contact Faf if they would like to check in on progress / scheduling.
  • Faf's public queue is always available for those that want to check for progress without active contact.

Client Benefits

  • Client models are backed up via cloud synchronization service (sync.com).
  • Returning clients will have commission application priority for model updates or new model commissions (not guaranteed if Faf is very booked).
  • Returning clients are allowed request for flexible payment plan options.
